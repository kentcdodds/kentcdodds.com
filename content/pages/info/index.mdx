## Kent's Name

These are correct:

- Kent
- Kent C.
- Kent C. Dodds
- Dodds

This is _**not**_ correct:

- Kent Dodds
- Kent Dodd
- Kent Dobbs
- Kent C. Dobbs
- Kent C Dodds (missing the `.`)
- Kent Dogs
- Ken

And if you want to add a possessive apostrophe to "Dodds" then it should be:
`Dodds'` not `Dodd's` nor `Dodds's`.
[Learn more](https://www.grammarbook.com/punctuation/apostro.asp)

engineer. I'm actively involved in the
[open source community](https://github.com/kentcdodds). I am the creator of

[the Epic Stack](https://epicweb.dev/epic-stack),
[EpicReact.dev](https://epicreact.dev), and
[TestingJavaScript.com](https://testingjavascript.com). I'm a
[Microsoft MVP](https://kcd.im/mvp), instructor on
[egghead.io](https://kcd.im/egghead) and [Frontend Masters](https://kcd.im/fem),
[live streamer](https://www.youtube.com/c/kentcdodds-vids),
and [podcaster](https://kentcdodds.com/calls). I'm married and the father of
five kids and I live in Utah.
