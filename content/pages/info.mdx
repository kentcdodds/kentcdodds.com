## Kent's Name

These are correct:

- Kent
- Kent C.
- Kent C. Dodds
- Dodds

This is _**not**_ correct:

- Kent Dodds
- Kent Dodd
- Kent Dobbs
- Kent C. Dobbs
- Kent C Dodds (missing the `.`)
- Kent Dogs

And if you want to add a possessive apostrophe to "Dodds" then it should be:
`Dodds'` not `Dodd's` nor `Dodds's`.
[Learn more](https://www.grammarbook.com/punctuation/apostro.asp)

## Bio

### Long First Person

Hi, I'm Kent C. Dodds. I'm a world renowned speaker, teacher, and trainer and
I'm actively involved in the
[open source community](https://github.com/kentcdodds) as a maintainer and
contributor of hundreds of popular npm packages. I'm a Co-Founder and Director of Developer Experience at [Remix](https://remix.run). I am the creator of
[EpicReact.Dev](https://epicreact.dev) and
[TestingJavaScript.com](https://testingjavascript.com). I'm an instructor on
[egghead.io](https://egghead.io/instructors/kentcdodds) and
[Frontend Masters](https://frontendmasters.com). I'm also a
[Google Developer Expert](https://developers.google.com/experts/people/kent-c-dodds).
I am happily married and the father of four kids. I like my family, code,
JavaScript, and React.
